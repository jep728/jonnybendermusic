ABOUT JONNY BENDER

Jonny Bender comes from the hair metal days of the late 80's - having played drums since the age of 5, learning guitar, bass and keys through developing recording - something he has been doing since 1995 - With a studio in downtown Tulsa, he had the privilege of working for Jamie Oldaker and Steve Ripley in the late 90's, soaking up every bit of mentorship possible. This iteration is the live, acoustic performer version of the old man....
